## Ongoing Review Service

A recommendation should not be left untouched indefinitely.

Annual and periodic reviews form an important part of the advisory approach, especially where investments, retirement income, and living annuities are involved.

## Why reviews matter

Personal circumstances change. Markets move. Income needs shift. Legislation evolves.

A review helps confirm whether an existing arrangement still serves its purpose or whether an adjustment should be considered.

## A review may lead to

- confirmation that the current arrangement remains appropriate,
- a recommendation to adjust part of the strategy,
- or the start of a new advice cycle where more substantial change is needed.

## Review focus areas

Reviews may consider investment performance in context, withdrawal sustainability, changes in personal circumstances, beneficiary considerations, administration issues, and any material developments that affect suitability.
