## Advice Process Overview

The advice process is designed to provide a consistent, professional, and client-focused experience from first contact through implementation and ongoing review.

<div className="process-grid">
  <div className="process-card"><h3>1. First Contact</h3><p>Initial contact establishes the nature of the enquiry and whether a meeting is appropriate.</p></div>
  <div className="process-card"><h3>2. Client Onboarding</h3><p>Client acceptance, AdviceTech setup, and supporting documentation are completed properly.</p></div>
  <div className="process-card"><h3>3. Financial Analysis</h3><p>The current position is reviewed to identify needs, gaps, and planning considerations.</p></div>
  <div className="process-card"><h3>4. Recommendation</h3><p>Suitable recommendations are prepared and explained with appropriate supporting information.</p></div>
  <div className="process-card"><h3>5. Implementation</h3><p>Agreed actions are submitted, tracked, and confirmed once active.</p></div>
  <div className="process-card"><h3>6. Annual Reviews</h3><p>Ongoing reviews help ensure arrangements remain suitable over time.</p></div>
</div>
